About Tony Prem Service Station

For over 25 years, Tony Prem Service Station has been providing top-quality automotive repair and maintenance services to our community. We pride ourselves on our dedication to customer satisfaction and expert workmanship.

Our team of certified mechanics has the skills and experience to handle all makes and models, using the latest diagnostic equipment and quality parts to ensure your vehicle runs at its best.

At Tony Prem Service Station, we believe in transparent pricing, honest advice, and building long-term relationships with our customers. That's why so many of our clients have been with us for years and continue to trust us with their vehicles.
