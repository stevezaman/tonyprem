About Us

Our Story

Tony Prem Service Station was founded in 1998 by Tony Prem, a master mechanic with over 15 years of experience working in dealerships and independent shops. Tony had a vision to create a service station that combined technical expertise with honest, transparent customer service.

What began as a small three-bay garage has grown into a full-service automotive center with state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and a team of certified technicians. Throughout our growth, we've remained true to Tony's original vision: providing quality repairs at fair prices with a focus on building long-term relationships with our customers.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of satisfied customers in the community. Many of our first-time customers become lifelong clients, trusting us with all their vehicles for years to come. We believe this loyalty stems from our unwavering commitment to integrity, quality workmanship, and exceptional service.

Our Mission

To provide honest, reliable, and high-quality automotive repair and maintenance services that ensure the safety and satisfaction of our customers while building long-term relationships based on trust and transparency.
